— Phase 1 · Warm-Up (8 min)
Treadmill Walk — Easy Pace
Warm-Up
🖥 Machine: Treadmill
4.5km/h
5 minDuration
0%Incline
Steps
  1. Step onto the treadmill and clip the safety key to your shirt.
  2. Set speed to 4.5 km/h and incline to 0%.
  3. Walk upright, shoulders back, arms swinging naturally.
  4. Breathe steadily — you should be able to talk easily.
  5. After 5 min, do 10 arm swings forward/back + 10 arm circles each direction.
— Phase 2 · Main Workout (45 min)
1. Flat Dumbbell Chest Press
Chest
🖥 Equipment: Flat bench + 2 dumbbells (10–12 kg each)
4Sets
10Reps
2-1-2Tempo
75sRest
Steps
  1. Sit on the end of a flat bench, a dumbbell resting on each thigh.
  2. Lie back, using your thighs to kick the dumbbells up to shoulder level.
  3. Plant feet flat on the floor, keep your back flat on the bench.
  4. Start with arms extended up, dumbbells above your chest, palms forward.
  5. Lower slowly for 2 counts until level with your chest, elbows at 45°.
  6. Pause 1 count — feel the stretch in your chest.
  7. Push up for 2 counts (don't lock elbows hard).
  8. Do 10 reps. Rest 75 sec, then next set. 4 sets total.
Form cue: Don't bounce the weight off your chest. Keep wrists straight.
2. Incline Dumbbell Press
Upper Chest
🖥 Equipment: Adjustable bench (30–45°) + 2 dumbbells (8–10 kg)
3Sets
10Reps
2-1-2Tempo
75sRest
Steps
  1. Set the bench backrest to a 30–45° incline.
  2. Sit down and kick the dumbbells up to your shoulders.
  3. Press them up so arms are extended above your upper chest.
  4. Lower slowly (2 counts) to the sides of your upper chest.
  5. Pause 1 count, then press up for 2 counts.
  6. Do 10 reps, rest 75 sec, 3 sets total.
Form cue: Use lighter weight than flat press. Don't arch your lower back.
3. Pec Deck / Machine Chest Flye
Chest
🖥 Machine: Pec Deck (butterfly) machine
3Sets
12Reps
60sRest
Steps
  1. Adjust the seat so the handles are at chest height.
  2. Sit with back flat against the pad, feet flat on floor.
  3. Grab both handles, arms out wide with a slight bend in the elbows.
  4. Squeeze your chest to bring the handles together in front of you.
  5. Hold 1 second when they nearly touch — squeeze hard.
  6. Slowly return (2 counts), feeling the chest stretch. 12 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Light weight. This is a squeeze/shaping move, not a strength lift.
4. Tricep Pushdown
Triceps
🖥 Machine: Cable machine + rope or straight-bar
3Sets
12Reps
1-1-2Tempo
60sRest
Steps
  1. Attach a rope or bar to the high pulley of the cable machine.
  2. Grab it with both hands, stand upright, feet shoulder-width.
  3. Pin your elbows tight to your sides — they stay fixed throughout.
  4. Push the bar down (1 count) until arms are fully straight.
  5. Hold 1 count at the bottom — squeeze the triceps.
  6. Slowly let it rise back to chest level (2 counts). 12 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Only forearms move. If elbows drift forward, lower the weight.
5. Overhead Tricep Extension
Triceps
🖥 Equipment: 1 dumbbell (8–10 kg)
3Sets
10Reps
60sRest
Steps
  1. Sit or stand holding one dumbbell vertically with both hands.
  2. Raise it straight overhead, arms extended.
  3. Keep elbows pointing forward and close to your head.
  4. Lower the dumbbell behind your head (2 counts) until forearms touch biceps.
  5. Press back up to the top (2 counts), squeezing triceps. 10 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Keep your core tight so you don't arch your back. Only forearms move.

Cool Down

— Phase 1 · Warm-Up (8 min)
Treadmill Brisk Walk + Cat-Cow
Warm-Up
🖥 Machine: Treadmill
5.5km/h
6 minDuration
1%Incline
Steps
  1. Walk on the treadmill at 5.5 km/h, 1% incline, for 6 minutes.
  2. Get on all fours. Arch your back up like a cat (exhale).
  3. Then drop your belly and lift your head (inhale). That's 1 Cat-Cow rep.
  4. Do 10 slow reps to loosen your spine before pulling.
  5. Finish with 10 seated torso rotations, hands behind head.
— Phase 2 · Main Workout (45 min)
1. Lat Pulldown
Back — Lats
🖥 Machine: Lat Pulldown (cable), wide bar
4Sets
10Reps
2-1-2Tempo
75sRest
Steps
  1. Adjust the thigh pad so your legs are locked snugly underneath.
  2. Grab the wide bar with an overhand grip, hands wider than shoulders.
  3. Sit down, lean back about 10°, chest up.
  4. Pull the bar down (2 counts) to your upper chest, driving elbows down and back.
  5. Squeeze your lats 1 count at the bottom.
  6. Let the bar rise slowly (2 counts) until arms are nearly straight. 10 reps, 4 sets.
Form cue: Don't yank with arms or swing your body. Think "pull elbows to hips."
2. Seated Cable Row
Back — Mid
🖥 Machine: Seated row (cable), V-handle
4Sets
10Reps
1-2-2Tempo
75sRest
Steps
  1. Sit on the bench, feet on the platform, knees slightly bent.
  2. Grab the V-handle, sit tall with a straight back.
  3. Pull the handle to your belly button (1 count), elbows driving straight back.
  4. Hold 2 counts, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  5. Slowly extend arms forward (2 counts), keeping your back upright. 10 reps, 4 sets.
Form cue: Don't round your back or lean far back. Keep the torso still.
3. Single-Arm Dumbbell Row
Back
🖥 Equipment: Flat bench + 1 dumbbell (14–18 kg)
3Sets
10Each side
60sRest
Steps
  1. Place your right knee and right hand on a flat bench.
  2. Left foot on the floor, back flat and parallel to the ground.
  3. Hold the dumbbell in your left hand, arm hanging straight down.
  4. Pull the dumbbell up to your hip (1 count), leading with your elbow.
  5. Squeeze at the top, then lower slowly (2 counts).
  6. Do 10 reps, switch sides. That's 1 set. 3 sets total.
Form cue: Pull toward your hip, not your shoulder. Keep your back flat — don't twist.
4. Dumbbell Bicep Curl
Biceps
🖥 Equipment: 2 dumbbells (10–12 kg each)
3Sets
12Reps
1-1-3Tempo
60sRest
Steps
  1. Stand tall, a dumbbell in each hand, arms at your sides, palms forward.
  2. Keep elbows pinned at your sides.
  3. Curl both dumbbells up (1 count) toward your shoulders.
  4. Squeeze 1 count at the top.
  5. Lower very slowly for 3 counts — this is where muscle is built.
  6. Do 12 reps, rest 60 sec, 3 sets.
Form cue: No swinging or using your back. If you swing, it's too heavy.
5. Hammer Curl
Biceps + Forearm
🖥 Equipment: 2 dumbbells (10–12 kg each)
3Sets
10Each arm
60sRest
Steps
  1. Stand holding dumbbells with a neutral grip — thumbs pointing up.
  2. Keep this grip throughout (like holding two hammers).
  3. Curl one dumbbell up toward your shoulder (1 count).
  4. Lower it slowly (2 counts), then curl the other arm.
  5. Alternate arms for 10 reps each. 3 sets.
Form cue: Targets the outer bicep and forearm. Elbows stay still.

Incline Treadmill Walk + Hip Circles

— Phase 2 · Main Workout (50 min)
1. Leg Press
Quads + Glutes
🖥 Machine: 45° Leg Press machine
4Sets
12Reps
3-1-2Tempo
90sRest
Steps
  1. Sit in the machine, back and head flat against the pad.
  2. Place feet shoulder-width on the platform, mid-foot.
  3. Push the platform up and release the safety handles.
  4. Lower slowly (3 counts) until your knees reach about 90°.
  5. Pause 1 count, then push back up (2 counts) through your heels.
  6. Don't lock your knees at the top. 12 reps, 4 sets, rest 90 sec.
Form cue: Knees track in line with your toes — don't let them cave inward.
2. Goblet Squat
Quads + Glutes + Core
🖥 Equipment: 1 dumbbell (14–20 kg)
3Sets
10Reps
3-1-1Tempo
75sRest
Steps
  1. Hold one dumbbell vertically against your chest, both hands cupping the top.
  2. Stand with feet shoulder-width, toes slightly pointed out.
  3. Keep your chest tall and core braced.
  4. Lower slowly (3 counts) by pushing hips back and bending knees, until thighs are parallel.
  5. Pause 1 count at the bottom.
  6. Drive up through your heels (1 count) to standing. 10 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Heels flat on the floor. Knees track over toes, chest stays up.
3. Lying Leg Curl
Hamstrings
🖥 Machine: Lying Leg Curl machine
3Sets
12Reps
1-1-3Tempo
60sRest
Steps
  1. Lie face down on the machine, ankles under the padded roller.
  2. Adjust the roller so it sits just above your heels.
  3. Grip the handles and keep your hips pressed into the pad.
  4. Curl your heels up toward your glutes (1 count).
  5. Squeeze 1 count at the top.
  6. Lower very slowly (3 counts). 12 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Don't lift your hips off the pad. Control the negative.
4. Standing Calf Raise
Calves
🖥 Machine: Calf raise / Smith machine, or a step
3Sets
15Reps
1-2-2Tempo
45sRest
Steps
  1. Stand with the balls of your feet on the edge of a platform or step.
  2. Let your heels drop down below the step for a full stretch.
  3. Rise up onto your toes as high as you can (1 count).
  4. Hold 2 counts at the top, squeezing the calves.
  5. Lower slowly (2 counts) back to the stretched position. 15 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Full range of motion is key. Don't bounce.
5. Plank
Core
🧍 Bodyweight — mat only
3Sets
30sHold
45sRest
Steps
  1. Get on the mat with forearms flat, elbows under your shoulders.
  2. Extend your legs back, balancing on your toes.
  3. Form a straight line from head to heels — squeeze glutes and abs.
  4. Hold for 30 seconds, breathing normally.
  5. Rest 45 sec, repeat for 3 sets.
Form cue: Don't let hips sag or pike up. Add 5 sec each week — target 60s by week 4.
6. Dead Bug
Core — Deep
🧍 Bodyweight — mat only
3Sets
8Each side
45sRest
Steps
  1. Lie on your back, arms pointing straight up to the ceiling.
  2. Lift legs so knees are bent at 90° (shins parallel to floor).
  3. Press your lower back flat into the floor — keep it there.
  4. Slowly lower your right arm overhead AND straighten your left leg (4 counts).
  5. Return to start, then do the opposite arm/leg. That's 1 rep each side.
  6. Do 8 each side, 3 sets.
Form cue: If your back arches off the floor, slow down or reduce range.
THURSDAY
🌿 Active Rest

REST & RECOVER

Muscles grow during rest, not the workout. Today is as important as gym days. Stay lightly active — no heavy lifting.

🚶

Light Walk

20–30 min slow walk outside. Keeps blood flowing without taxing muscles.

💧

Hydrate

Aim for 3–4 litres of water. Recovery needs hydration.

🧘

Stretch

10–15 min light stretching of sore areas from Mon–Wed.

😴

Sleep

7–9 hours. 80% of muscle repair happens during sleep.

🍗

Eat Protein

Chicken, eggs, dal, paneer. Muscles repair right now.

🚫

Avoid

Heavy lifting, intense cardio, alcohol.

Shoulders

4Sets
10Reps

Steps
  1. Set a bench upright and sit with your back fully supported.

  4. Pause 1 count at the top.

2. Dumbbell Lateral Raise
Side Delts
🖥 Equipment: 2 LIGHT dumbbells (5–6 kg)
4Sets
12Reps
2-1-3Tempo
60sRest
Steps
  1. Stand tall, a light dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
  2. Keep a slight bend in your elbows.
  3. Raise both arms out to the sides (2 counts) until at shoulder height.
  4. Pause 1 count — pinkies slightly higher than thumbs.
  5. Lower very slowly (3 counts). 12 reps, 4 sets.
Form cue: Light weight — this is the #1 shoulder-width builder. Don't shrug or swing.
3. Face Pull
Rear Delts + Rotator Cuff
🖥 Machine: Cable machine + rope, set at face height
3Sets
15Reps
60sRest
Steps
  1. Set the cable pulley to face height and attach the rope.
  2. Grab both rope ends, step back so there's tension, lean back slightly.
  3. Pull the rope toward your face, splitting it apart at your ears.
  4. Elbows flare out high and wide — squeeze your rear delts.
  5. Slowly return (2 counts). 15 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Light weight. Protects shoulders and fixes posture — don't skip it.
4. Dumbbell Shrugs
Traps
🖥 Equipment: 2 dumbbells (20–24 kg each)
3Sets
12Reps
1-2-2Tempo
60sRest
Steps
  1. Stand tall holding a heavy dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
  2. Keep arms straight (they don't bend).
  3. Shrug your shoulders straight UP toward your ears (1 count).
  4. Hold 2 counts at the top, squeezing the traps.
  5. Lower slowly (2 counts). 12 reps, 3 sets.
Form cue: Shrug straight up — never roll your shoulders, it strains the joints.

— Phase 2 · Full Body Circuit (35 min)
📋 CIRCUIT FORMAT: Do all 5 exercises back-to-back with 30s rest between each. After all 5, rest 2 min. Repeat the full circuit 3 times.
A. Dumbbell Thruster (Squat to Press)
Full Body
🖥 Equipment: 2 dumbbells (8–10 kg each)
3Rounds
10Reps
Steps
  1. Hold a dumbbell at each shoulder, feet shoulder-width.
  2. Squat down until thighs are parallel.
  3. As you stand up, press both dumbbells overhead in one smooth motion.
  4. Lower the dumbbells back to shoulders and go straight into the next squat.
  5. Do 10 reps. Rest 30 sec, move to exercise B.
B. Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
Hamstrings + Glutes
🖥 Equipment: 2 dumbbells (14–18 kg each)
3Rounds
10Reps
Steps
  1. Stand holding dumbbells in front of your thighs, knees slightly bent.
  2. Push your hips back and hinge forward, lowering the dumbbells down your legs.
  3. Go until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings (around shin level).
  4. Keep your back flat the entire time — never round it.
  5. Drive hips forward to stand up. 10 reps, rest 30 sec, go to C.
Form cue: This is a hip hinge, NOT a squat. Movement comes from hips going back.
C. Bent-Over Dumbbell Row
Back
🖥 Equipment: 2 dumbbells
3Rounds
10Reps
Steps
  1. Hinge forward at the hips about 45°, back flat, dumbbells hanging down.
  2. Row both dumbbells up to your sides, elbows driving back.
  3. Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top.
  4. Lower slowly. 10 reps, rest 30 sec, go to D.
D. Push-Up (or Knee Push-Up)
Chest + Triceps
🧍 Bodyweight
3Rounds
8–12Reps
Steps
  1. Place hands slightly wider than shoulders, body in a straight line.
  2. (If full push-ups are too hard, drop to your knees — totally fine.)
  3. Lower your chest toward the floor (2 counts), elbows at 45°.
  4. Push back up to the top. Do as many as you can with good form (8–12).
  5. Rest 30 sec, go to E.
E. Mountain Climbers
Core + Cardio
🧍 Bodyweight
3Rounds
20Total
2 minRest after
Steps
  1. Get into a push-up position, hands under shoulders, body straight.
  2. Drive your right knee toward your chest, then return it.
  3. Immediately drive your left knee in. Keep alternating at a controlled pace.
  4. Do 20 total (10 each leg). Then rest 2 min before repeating the whole circuit.
Form cue: Keep hips low and level — don't let your butt pop up.
