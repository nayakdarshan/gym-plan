THURSDAY
🌿 Active Rest

REST & RECOVER

Muscles grow during rest, not during the workout. Today is just as important as gym days. Stay lightly active but no heavy lifting.

🚶

Light Walk

20–30 min slow walk outside. Keeps blood flowing without taxing muscles.

💧

Hydrate

Aim for 3–4 litres of water. Recovery requires hydration.

🧘

Stretch

10–15 min light stretching. Focus on sore areas from Mon–Wed.

😴

Sleep

7–9 hours. 80% of muscle repair happens during sleep.

🍗

Eat Protein

Chicken, eggs, dal, paneer. Muscles are repairing right now.

🚫

Avoid

Heavy lifting, intense cardio, alcohol. Don't undo your week.
